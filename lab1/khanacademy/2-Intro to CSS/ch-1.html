Life of a Puma

The puma is a wild and powerful animal that lives in North and South America. It is also called a mountain lion or cougar. The puma is very fast and strong, which helps it hunt for food.

Pumas live in forests, mountains, and deserts. They usually live alone and are most active at night. Their main food is deer and small animals.

The puma is an important part of nature because it helps keep the animal population balanced.
