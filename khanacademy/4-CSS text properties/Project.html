Second post

Published January 23, 2026

Lately, I have been diving deeper into the world of digital technologies and learning. I am studying the basics of web development, learning how to work with HTML and CSS, trying to change headings, adjust styles, and understand how elements on a page work. At the same time, I am learning programming and databases to create useful projects, such as electronic directories and information storage systems.

It is also important for me to understand the ethics of digital design — how to create technologies that respect human rights, protect data, and make the digital environment safer and more fair. Sometimes I translate texts, study English, and try to write simply and clearly so I can share knowledge with others.

Besides studying, I enjoy writing short texts for advertising and blogs — for example, about travel, such as tours to Egypt, or about interesting facts about the world around us. For me, this is a way to develop creativity and learn to express my thoughts clearly and beautifully.

This path is about constant growth, curiosity, and the desire to become better in the digital world
